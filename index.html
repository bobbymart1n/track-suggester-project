What track should you go into?

You got Front End Development

, you might be interested in the Front End Development track! Also known as the CSS/React track, which is all about design/aesthetic and functionality of websites or applications. You got Back End Development

Everyone has to take part in an Intro to Programming course before your designated track begins. In this intro class you will learn about basic HTML, CSS, JS, and basic programming principles to get a base line understanding on the essentials of web development.
